How Flux Works

Discover how Flux transforms your inbox into an AI-powered automation engine that handles support tickets, inquiries, and workflows with sub-second latency.

Step 1

Connect Your Gmail

Start by securely connecting your Gmail account using OAuth 2.0 authentication. Flux never stores your password and uses bank-grade encryption to protect your data.

  • One-click OAuth connection
  • Enterprise-grade security
  • Instant agent activation
  • No password required

Step 2

Monitor Your Dashboard

Get real-time insights into your email processing with beautiful charts and metrics. Track performance, analyze trends, and understand your email patterns at a glance.

  • Real-time email volume charts
  • Category distribution analytics
  • Response rate tracking
  • Custom date range filtering

Step 3

Track Every Email

View detailed logs of every email processed by Flux. Filter by status, search by sender, and understand exactly how your agent is handling each message.

  • Comprehensive activity logs
  • Status filtering (Responded, Ignored, Failed)
  • Pagination for easy navigation
  • Detailed processing information

Step 4

Build Your Knowledge Base

Upload PDF documents to create a powerful RAG (Retrieval Augmented Generation) system. Flux uses your knowledge base to generate accurate, contextual responses.

  • Drag-and-drop PDF uploads
  • Automatic text chunking
  • Vector database storage
  • Semantic search capabilities

Step 5

Let AI Handle the Rest

The Flux AI agent continuously monitors your inbox, processes new emails, and drafts intelligent responses using your knowledge base. All on autopilot.

  • Real-time email monitoring
  • AI-powered response generation
  • Context-aware replies
  • Automatic categorization
